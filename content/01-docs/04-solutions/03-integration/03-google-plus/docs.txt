## Static Google Plus Sharing Button

```

<a class="button" href="https://plusone.google.com/_/+1/confirm?hl=de&url=<?php echo rawurlencode ($page->url()); ?>&title=<?php echo rawurlencode($page->title()); ?>" target="blank" title="Share on Google+">Share on Google+</a>

```

This link will share the current page on Google Plus.
