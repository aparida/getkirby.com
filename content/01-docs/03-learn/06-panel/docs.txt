Title: Admin Panel

----

Text:

The Kirby Panel is an open-source interface for your Kirby installation. It will give you and your clients the possibility to administrate a Kirby-flavoured site without touching the original files. It also makes it possible to edit content from a mobile device, while you are on the road.

The Kirby Panel is highly customizable.
Read more about how to get the best out of it here.

(image: 01.png link: https://github.com/getkirby/panel)

## Download
